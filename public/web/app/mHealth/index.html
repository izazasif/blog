About mHealth

ANDROID HEALTH APPLICATION THAT PROVIDS HOME REMEDIES & GENERAL TIPS FOR COMMON DISEASE & IT WILL ALSO PROVIDE 24/7 DOCTOR’S SUPPORT, ONLINE MEDICINE, PEDOMETER, AMBULANCE, BLOOD BANK & HOSPITAL INFO, ASK DOCTOR & MEDICINE REMINDER.

What Is mHealth:

mHEALTH is an app where you can experience a wide range of services that not only patients but any subscriber like you can benefit from, services that can make your life as a patient a whole lot easier.

Our goal at mHealth service is to give patient hassle-free access to electronic medical services that can help you save time, effort, and money.

mHEALTH is just the perfect application that provides home remedies and natural tips to prevent common disease. Beside that the application also provides 24/7 doctors service, Online medicine service, Pedometer, Hospital & Ambulance info, Question to physicians & Medicine reminder.

mHealth Benefits:

MIAKI CONTENT VAULT The app has beautiful and simple interface layout, where you can find multiple health related services in one application.

It contains 8 major features with huge of benefits. The major benefits are getting Health tips, directly talk to the doctors 24/7 just pressing single button, Can count the steps and keep the records for future, Ambulance, Blood bank & Hospital info including address and contact number from 8 district, Medicine, vaccination reminder & ask the expert.

mHEALTH App provides you Comprehensive Health Tips and Awareness for Healthy Life and Well-being.
