mHealth

The app has a beautiful and simple interface layout, where you can find multiple health-related services in one application. The features are very useful for both patients & users.

The application contains 8 major features with huge benefits. The major benefits are getting Health tips, directly talk to the doctors 24/7 just pressing single button, Can count the steps and keep the records for future, Ambulance, Blood bank & Hospital info including address and contact number from 8 districts, medicine, vaccination reminder & ask the expert

mHEALTH App provides you Comprehensive Health Tips and Awareness for Healthy Life and Well-being.
