About mChef

Introducing the most amazing recipe app mChef in Bangla! Designed to delight, mChef has been reinvented as the most popular food-focused social app with a large community of home cooks who play a part in helping cooks discover and share the joy of home cooking.

With our latest feature you are now able to publish your very own recipes to the mChef app! Take explaining photos of each recipe step and show other home cooks how to recreate your favorite dishes.
